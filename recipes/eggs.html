Creamy Scrambled Eggs


BACK

Description:

If your looking for a great way to start your day , Breakfast-wsie, try our cream, delicious scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • A tablespoon of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • A pan
  • 6 slices of bread (of any kind )

Steps:

  1. Toast the 6 slices of bread in the toaster
  2. Put the pan on medium-low heat
  3. Add the 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  4. Put the eggs in and mix them around
  5. Add in the butter
  6. Make sure you alternate keeping the pan on and off the heat in 20 minute intervals (while constantly stirring)
  7. Cook until to your liking
ENJOY!
