High Calorie Peanut Butter Bulking Shake

Description

Struggling to put on weight for the summer, or just want a tasty snack that will fill you up? Here is an easy, affordable, 1 000 calorie milkshake that will help you achieve your fitness goals

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • Half a cup of oats
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • A blender
  • Ice blocks
Steps:
  1. Blend the oats in the blender for 30 seconds
  2. Open the banana and break the pieces into the blender
  3. Put the peanut butter and the honney into the blender
  4. Pour the milk into the blender
  5. Add ice block into it (depending on how cold you want the shake )

ENJOY!
